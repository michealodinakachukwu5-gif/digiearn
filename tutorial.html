FAQ
Common Questions
How much can I earn daily? ▼
Basic members earn up to ₦500 per day. Classic members earn up to ₦2,000 per day. You can earn more through referrals and weekly spins.
When do tasks reset? ▼
Tasks reset every 24 hours at midnight. Come back every day to complete new tasks and earn more money.
What if I forget to do my tasks? ▼
No problem! You don't lose anything. Just log in the next day and your daily tasks will be available again. Consistency is key to reaching your withdrawal target faster.
Can I do tasks on my phone? ▼
Yes! DigiEarn works perfectly on any phone browser. Just open digiearn.name.ng on your phone and log in to your dashboard to complete tasks anytime.
How many people can I refer? ▼
There is no limit! Refer as many people as you want. Each referral earns you ₦500 (Basic) or ₦1,000 (Classic). The more you refer the faster you reach your withdrawal target.
Is DigiEarn legit and safe? ▼
Yes! DigiEarn is a legitimate Nigerian earning platform. Payments are processed by Paystack — Nigeria's most trusted payment gateway. All withdrawals go directly to verified Nigerian bank accounts.
What if I don't receive my withdrawal? ▼
Withdrawals are processed within 24 hours. If you don't receive payment after 24 hours, contact us at digiearn.name.ng. Make sure you requested during the withdrawal window (Mon–Fri, 10am–4pm).
